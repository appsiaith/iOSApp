The Course

Cymraeg i'r Teulu is a course for parents who want to learn to speak Welsh with children of Foundation Phase age (up to 7 years old). It is suitable for total beginners, or for those who have some previous knowledge of Welsh. There are north Wales and south Wales versions of the course, and the following resources exist:
  • Coursebook (parts 1 and 2)
  • Homework/Activity book (parts 1 and 2)
  • Board games pack
  • CD
  • Small picture cards
  • A4 Flashcards for tutors

This App

This App is a free sampler of the Cymraeg i'r Teulu course. It has the coursebook material for the first three chapters presented in a more interactive way. "Current Unit" lets you work through the different aspects of the material in the chapter - learning vocabulary and ways of saying things, then practicing that in exercises and recorded material.
